    About

    THE CRITICALLY ACCLAIMED FANTASY ACTION RPG Rise, Tarnished, and be guided by grace to brandish the power of the Elden Ring. • A Breathtaking World Full of Excitement and Mystery The Lands Between are part of a vast continent where magnificent open fields and huge dungeons with complex and three-dimensional designs are seamlessly connected. As you explore, the joy of discovering unknown and overwhelming threats awaits you. Mastery of the terrain and knowledge of its secrets can help you overcome enemies and defeat formidable bosses or lead invading players into traps.

    System Requirements

    Minimum Requirements

    • Requires: 64-bit processor and OS
    • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
    • Processor: Intel Core i5-8400 / AMD Ryzen 5 1600
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 6GB / AMD Radeon RX 580 8GB
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 130 GB available space
    • Sound Card: Windows Compatible Audio Device
    • Additional Notes: HDD Supported, SSD Recommended. Tested with DLSS/FSR/XeSS enabled.

    Recommended Requirements

    • Requires: 64-bit processor and OS
    • OS: Windows 10 64-bit
    • Processor: Intel Core i7-9700 / AMD Ryzen 5 5500
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
    • Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 2060 / AMD Radeon RX 5700 XT / INTEL Arc A750
    • DirectX: Version 12
    • Storage: 130 GB available space
    • Sound Card: Windows Compatible Audio Device
    • Additional Notes: SSD Required. Tested with DLSS/FSR/XeSS enabled.
